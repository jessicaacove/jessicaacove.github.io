A CREATIVE CONCEPT FOR CORAL REEF DONATIONS

AdobeXD X The Ocean Agency

AdobeXD teamed up with The Ocean Agency to host a Creative Jam with the goal of creating a digital experience using

AdobeXD

that serves as a platform to

collect donations

towards the agency's cause of coral reef restoration.

The Brief

The product needs...

a fun, easy way to donate

to educate about the cause

a way to share support

and opportunities for corporate involvement.

The Constraints

Due to time and schedule constraints, my partner Alexandra Jimenez and I had a three-day sprint to design the product. Oh yeah, and we had no prior experience with AdobeXD.

Research


THE OCEAN AGENCY



The Ocean Agency was created to advertise ocean issues and provide support for conservation efforts. Their notable past projects include the 2017 documentary Chasing Coral and their partnership with Google, taking viewers "virtual diving" via Google Maps.

THE SCIENCE OF DONATION

We began with research into why people donate to determine the users' needs.

People are more likely to give if they are asked by a friend.

Having a tangible outcome rather than an abstract statistic yields better results.

People are more likely to give if they perceive a high probability of the cause affecting them.

Martrydom effect dictates that people are more likely to give if they have made a sacrifice.

About 1 in 2 people feel that donating time is just as important as donating money.

OCEAN RESEARCH

Coral reefs are immensely important for:

  • Economic Impact

    Tourism related to coral reefs translates to billions of dollars and millions of jobs in over 100 countries around the world.

  • Medicine

    We are 300 to 400 times more likely to find new drugs from coral reef ecosystems than land-based ones.

  • Food

    Almost 1/5 of the world's protein comes from seafood.

  • Protection

    They act as natural barriers, canceling out 97% of a wave’s strength.

But, the statistic that made the most impact was

80% of the oxygen we breathe is produced by ocean ecosystems supported by coral reefs.

USER RESEARCH

We interviewed 10 people that best fit the general target user that was provided to us by The Ocean Agency: female leaning, with disposable income. We asked mainly open-ended questions about what compells donation and what features a user looks for when choosing a cause to support.

79% of users had not seen the Chasing Coral documentary.

The Problem


PAINS & GAINS



Transparency

Users want to know where their dollar goes

Connection

Users want to feel personally affected by the cause.

Engagement

Users want to be engaged in a fun, delightful experience.

IDEATION

How might we transparently communicate what a donation buys?

Demostrate the processes the agency is implementing to restore coral reefs.

Effectively explain what a dollar amount helps to accomplish.

How might we educate people as to how reefs affect them?

Provide statistics to show how coral reefs impact our daily lives.

Educate through platforms that users are already familiar with.

How might we create an engaging, fun, and delightfal experience?

Utilize the graphic photography and fun colors provided by The Ocean Agency in our design.

Gameify the experience by creating a viral challenge.
