Spotify: Add a Feature

Adding a new feature to an existing application

What is Spotify? The world's leading music streaming app by market share.

I chose Spotify because I was initially attracted to the dark UI. They have 217 million monthly active users and climbing, and I welcomed the challenge of finding a problem with such a successful product and fitting it in to a feature-ful interface.

The Goal...

... is to create a new feature that meets user needs and integrates seamlessly with the existing product.

Research

Through background research, I learned that 99% of all available streaming comes from just 10% of songs.

I wondered if that 10% were those featured on playlists and if users had difficulty locating music outside that 10%.

Business Analysis

A Competitive Feature Comparison helped me to identify users' mental models and expectations, as well as identify opportunities for design.*

I continued with a Market Positioning Chart. With my background research in mind, I wanted to see if the breadth of music offered and the organization of that music, played a role in positioning. If users only access 10% of music, do diverse offerings or organization play a role in a platform's popularity?

User Research

I did 10 interviews. I really had to dig deep; most users didn't think that they had any problems.

Remembering from my research that only 10% of music gets streamed, I asked my users about ways that they discover new music.
